## Project Overview
This project explores an AI-powered "second brain" system designed to capture fleeting thoughts and ideas with zero friction. The core innovation lies in using AI to synthesize disparate thoughts into meaningful themes, identify recurring patterns, and discover non-obvious connections across time periods and contexts. The system aims to augment human creativity by surfacing "clusters of energy" where users demonstrate natural momentum and interest.

### ai-opportunity-assessment
- **second-brain-brainstorm.md**: An AI opportunity concept for a thought capture and synthesis system that identifies patterns, themes, and unexpected connections in user ideas using advanced pattern recognition.

## Key Themes
- **AI-Powered Pattern Recognition**: The system leverages AI to move beyond simple note-taking, instead identifying recurring themes, contradictions in thinking, and clusters of related ideas that reveal where users have natural momentum and energy.
- **Frictionless Idea Capture**: The design prioritizes zero-friction capture of fleeting thoughts, recognizing that human creativity often emerges from random, ephemeral ideas that must be quickly recorded before they're lost.
- **Creative Augmentation Through Synthesis**: Rather than replacing human thinking, the system enhances human creativity by discovering non-obvious relationships and connections that users might miss on their own.
